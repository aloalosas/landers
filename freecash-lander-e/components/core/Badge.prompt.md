Tiny inline labels — brand wordmark, step counters, and scarcity chips.

```jsx
<Badge variant="logo">Freecash App</Badge>
<Badge variant="step">1 / 2</Badge>
<Badge variant="tint">NEW</Badge>
<Badge variant="alert">⚡ Limited spots</Badge>
```

Variants: `logo` (wordmark + glowing dot; pass `white` for the dark-on-light step-complete state) · `step` (muted pill) · `tint` (green chip) · `alert` (gold viral pill).
